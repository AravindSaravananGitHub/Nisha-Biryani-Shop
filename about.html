About Us

For over 10 years, we've been serving authentic, homemade-style biryanis that bring the taste of home to your plate. Starting with our first branch in Marimalai Nagar, we’ve expanded to Tambaram, Tidel, and near the Tidle Park. Founded by Chef Nisha R, our shop is known for its rich flavors, quality ingredients, and heartfelt recipes. Join us for a biryani experience that’s as warm and welcoming as home.
